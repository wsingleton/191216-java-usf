Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Directions

    type="i"
  1. Add all Ingredients into a high speed blender
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes
  3. Strain oute excees plant matter within cheesecloth
  4. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days
