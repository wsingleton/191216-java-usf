Iframes

An iframe is like a little window that has been place into your webpage. The term "iframe" and an abbr for "inline frame".

One common use of iframes is to embed Google maps into webpage. The content of the iframe can be any HTML page (which can be located either externally or locally on out machine/wbeserver)
