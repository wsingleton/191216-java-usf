Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salf
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add alll ingreds into blender
  2. Blend on high fro 1-2 minutes
  3. Strain out excess plant matter with cheesecloth
  4. Stror in fridge
