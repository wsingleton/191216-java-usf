Homemade Hemp Mil

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 txp of maple syrup

Directions
  1. Add all Ingredients inot a high-syrup
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 min
  3. Strain out excess plant matter with cheeseclot
  4. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
