Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1.4 rsp of sea salf
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Direction

  1. add all ingredients into a high-speed blender
  2. blend on hight for about 2 mins
  3. strain out excess plant matter with cheesecloth
  4. Store in the fridfe for up to 5 days
